# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Amazon operators, product researchers, and listing specialists who need fast pre-launch buyer-reaction hypotheses for the US marketplace.

## Product Purpose

This desktop tool simulates structured feedback from predefined US consumer personas after the user enters product and category information. Success means the operator can identify likely purchase intent, objection patterns, listing gaps, image needs, and bad-review risks before investing in inventory, listing production, or A+ content.

## Brand Personality

Analytical, restrained, practical. The product should feel like a focused research workstation, not a marketing toy.

## Anti-references

Avoid fake-review generation, exaggerated AI claims, gamified survey visuals, cluttered enterprise dashboards, and decorative AI gradients.

## Design Principles

- Keep research provenance explicit: simulated persona output is an operational hypothesis, not real consumer evidence.
- Push users from vague product ideas to structured inputs and measurable outputs.
- Surface risks as strongly as opportunities.
- Make saved research easy to revisit, compare, and delete.
- Keep the workflow dense but calm for repeated operator use.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, and reduced-motion-safe transitions.
